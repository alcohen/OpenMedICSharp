Class Samples

This is a variable-size array of Sample objects. Its purpose is to initialize to a fixed max. size, then have its Sample objs. be updated without re-instantiating them each time.

The "current size" of the array is a window of the desired size into a subset of the internal array; again, resizing does not require re-instantiation.

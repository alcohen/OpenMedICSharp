FileWriter.writeValuesAsAscii

Whether the data values are written as ASCII character or as binary values. A float value will occupy up to 14 ASCII characters (i.e. 14 bytes on disk), but always exactly 4 bytes as a binary. ASCII has the advantage of being readable by humans and many other programs, such as spreadsheet programs.

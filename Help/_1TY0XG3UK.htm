Class WfdbAccess

PhysioNet is an Internet resource for biomedical research and development sponsored by the NIH's National Center for Research Resources. PhysioNet, PhysioBank, WFDB, and PhysioToolkit are the product of collaborative efforts by numerous people, too numerous to mention here; please visit the PhysioNet website (www.physionet.org) for details. The term "Wfdb" is used throughout to indicate the suite of functions included in the WFDB Library. This is a static class to simplify and/or document the accessing of the WFDB methods in wfdb-sharp.dll (namespace Wfdb).
